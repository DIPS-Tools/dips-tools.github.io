[DataPACT](https://datapact.eu/) focuses on revolutionising data/AI operations by integrating **compliance, privacy, and environmental sustainability** into their core design — making them foundational elements of data/AI pipelines rather than afterthoughts.

The project addresses the challenges posed by new AI models, ever-increasing data volumes, complex AI systems, and a multitude of emerging legislation such as the GDPR, the Data (Governance) Act and the AI Act. DataPACT delivers three key resources:

- a **Compliance Toolbox** and a **Compliance Framework** for assessing data/AI pipelines, and
- a **data/AI pipeline management toolbox** for building compliance-aware pipelines.

DataPACT supports the open European data spaces, helping organisations create fair, privacy-respecting and transparent data-driven systems. Our contribution builds on the lab's line of work on consent management in databases and data workflows {% cite konstantinidis2021consent filipczuk2024graph %}.

**Official website:** [datapact.eu](https://datapact.eu/)

DataPACT is funded by the European Union's Horizon Europe research and innovation programme under grant agreement [No 101189771](https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/101189771), with a consortium of 19 partners.
